2Phase 2: The "Extension" Menu

Satisfy the need for personalization using the "Add to Cart" psychology.

  • Don't ask: "What do you want?"
  • Do ask: "For the Malvern 20, we offer a 'Modern Chef Kitchen' upgrade. It adds pot drawers and stone benchtops for exactly $5,400. Shall we add that?"

3Phase 3: The Site Start Anchor

Secure the $2,000 Groundwork fee using scarcity.

"Because our admin process is so fast, we book construction slots tight. Paying the groundwork fee today locks in your start month."

3. Modern Tactics for 2025

Digital-First Visualization

If you don't have a render of the specific house, use the Display Proxy technique:

"This kitchen layout is identical to our Display in [Location], just mirrored."

"Lifestyle" Selling

Stop selling bricks. Sell the Saturday morning.

  • Bad: "This house has a 3x3m alfresco."
  • Good: "This alfresco is pre-designed to fit a 6-seater setting perfect for your weekend BBQs, without eating up your whole backyard."

4. Handling Objections

"I want to move the bathroom wall."

Response: We can do a Custom build, but that adds ~$20k and 4 months. Most clients find the Signature Ensuite upgrade ($3,500) gives that luxury feel without the custom price tag. Should I show you that?

"I want to choose my own tiles."

Response: That’s actually how we keep our build times so fast. By using our curated range, we guarantee stock availability. If you pick a custom tile that goes out of stock, your house sits empty. We prioritize getting you in sooner.
