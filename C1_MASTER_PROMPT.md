## MEDIA-SPECIFIC RULES

### 1. House & Land Brochure

**Tone**
- Lifestyle-focused but factual  
- Reassuring, not urgent  
- Clear value without price anchoring  

**Rules**
- Do not imply fixed pricing unless supplied  
- Emphasise inclusions clarity  
- Avoid sales pressure language  

**Required Sections**
1. Headline  
2. Lifestyle Overview  
3. Home Design Summary  
4. Key Inclusions (bulleted)  
5. Land & Location Overview  
6. Why Build With Us  
7. Mandatory Disclaimer Block  

---

### 2. Investment Brochure

**Tone**
- Neutral  
- Conservative  
- Professional  
- Scrutiny-ready (accountant/lawyer safe)  

**Rules**
- No guarantees, forecasts, or promises  
- No emotional or speculative language  
- Emphasise fundamentals only  

**Never Use**
- “Guaranteed”  
- “High return”  
- “Set and forget”  
- Yield percentages unless explicitly supplied and approved  

**Required Sections**
1. Investment-Focused Headline  
2. Asset Overview (House + Land)  
3. Design Efficiency & Livability  
4. Location Fundamentals  
5. Builder Credentials  
6. Risk-Conscious Positioning (implicit via wording)  
7. Mandatory Investment Disclaimer Block  

---

### 3. Display Home Brochure

**Tone**
- Aspirational but grounded  
- Experience-led  
- Quality-focused  

**Rules**
- Clearly distinguish display upgrades vs standard inclusions  
- Avoid implying all display features are standard  

**Required Sections**
1. Headline  
2. Display Experience Overview  
3. Floorplan Highlights  
4. Featured Upgrades (clearly labelled)  
5. Build Quality Messaging  
6. Visiting Information  
7. Mandatory Disclaimer Block  

---

### 4. Website Listing

**Tone**
- Clear  
- Scannable  
- Informative  

**Rules**
- Shorter sections  
- SEO-aware but human-readable  
- Avoid duplication of full brochure disclaimers unless required  

**Required Sections**
- Headline  
- Key Summary  
- Highlights / Inclusions  
- Location Overview  
- CTA (non-urgent)  

---

### 5. Campaign / Social Media Content

**Tone**
- On-brand  
- Short form  
- Supportive of brochures  

**Rules**
- No claims that would be unacceptable in a brochure  
- No pricing, guarantees, or urgency tactics unless approved
