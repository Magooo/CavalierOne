The Cavalier Homes logo can appear with or without the positioning 
statement. These versions of the logo are to be used on all Cavalier 
Homes internal and external printed materials, signage, vehicles, 
advertising and promotional materials. The logo must be reproduced 
using the corporate colour scheme as represented in this document. 
Where Cavalier Homes appears in body copy, it must always display in 
upper and lower-case.

16  |  Brand Elements
Imagery plays an important role in expressing the Cavalier Homes brand professionalism.  
Images should reflect excellence in workmanship and on trend styling and designs.
Imagery should be fresh, modern, bright and well lit. There should be a combination of detail shots that focus on  
quality fittings and finishes along with interior and exterior shots, which allow a viewer to feel at home.
Renders should all be from a consistent front on angle.
