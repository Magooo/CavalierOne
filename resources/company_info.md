## Business Overview
**History:**
It’s in their DNA. There was never any doubt about what Scott and Jason McHugh would do with their lives. They come from generations of builders, and very early on, they knew they were going to be home builders. They shared the belief that honesty, strong work ethics and high-quality construction would be the foundation of their business. In short, they’d be fair dinkum with their clients and raise the bar for local building standards.

In 2003, they became part of Cavalier Homes — and it was important that their local reputation stayed strong. They were proud to represent a national building group, but just as proud to continue building for the Shepparton community and the broader Goulburn Valley.

Today, the strong reputation of Cavalier Homes Shepparton is well-established. The McHugh brothers continue to build with integrity, and their work spans across Shepparton, Echuca, and towns throughout the Goulburn and Murray districts. Whether you're looking to build your dream home in central Shepparton, a custom home in Kialla, or secure a house and land package in Shepparton’s growing estates, their team is ready to help.

So if you’re considering building in Cohuna, Alexandra, Glenrowan, Elmore or anywhere in between — Cavalier Homes Goulburn Valley is your trusted, experienced local choice.

## Market Position & Strategy
**Service Area:**
Primary: Shepparton, Kialla, Mooroopna.
Expansion: Echuca, Moama (targeting due to land availability), Tocumwal, Barham, Benalla, Yarrawonga.

**Target Market:**
- **Primary:** Middle-aged families, mid-to-high income, seeking custom "forever homes".
- **Secondary:** First Home Buyers (via speculative lower-end homes).
- **Government:** Social housing tenders (aiming for 1-2 per year, e.g., YWCA Housing).


**Competitors:**
- **Simonds Homes:** Servicing Shepparton region.
- **Metricon:** House & land packages in Shepparton North estates.
