Fast Keys Process
Sales Consultant Process;
Client chooses plan & fills out brochure
Sales consultant completes site inspection and sales estimate with site costs and chosen selections from brochure
Confirm with client that all floor plan extensions are done with sales consultant
$2,000 Groundwork paid and following documentation must be signed;
Brochure
Floor & site plan
Sales estimate
Plan of sub
Groundwork form
Sales file to be handed to Courtney for Sales Accept

Contract Admin Process;
Applies for soil test and energy rating (approximate 4 weeks)
Sends for plans to be completed
Once soil test, energy rating and plans are received, client is booked in for colour selection and complete both colours and precontract on the same day. 
Complete any plan changes in colours the following day. 
Complete the contract the day after the plan changes have been completed.
