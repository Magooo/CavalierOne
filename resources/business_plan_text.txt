Business Summa 

Business Overview 

Owners Scott & Jason McHugh have been operating in the Goulburn Valley with over 60 years of 
experience in building between them. Their father Bob started the business back in 1989 under McHugh 
Builders with Scott and Jason starting as Carpenter's apprentices and over the years promoting to the 
directors of the business they are today.  In 2003, they became part of the Cavalier Homes Franchise and 
are majority shareholders in the franchise.  In 2019 they opened an office in Echuca which was an obvious 
choice with the purpose of supporting local trades and suppliers and to increase their market share. 

Honesty and integrity are at the core of everything Cavalier Homes do.  Our commitment to transparent 
pricing means that our clients have no surprises and can guarantee the end result is a quality built house 
they are proud to call home. 

The Cavalier Homes team are made up of familiar faces from around the Echuca and Gou I burn Valley 
area, meaning they know all there is to know about the location and just about everything in between. 
Their expert knowledge follows into soil types, flood plains,  dry climates and most importantly, what sort 
of house suits your needs.  Cavalier Homes currently employs 42 people in the Gou I burn Valley area 
which includes highly skilled professionals, tradesman and apprentices. 

Giving a taste of the best of both worlds,  Cavalier Homes has all the big builder components whilst being 
a smaller franchise,  it is able to commit their direct time and expertise to their customers. 

Local is important to Cavalier Homes and from the initial meeting with the sales team to picking the 
perfect floor plan to suit your lifestyle and block of land. You can then be guided through the beautiful 
colour selection room all in the one local office,  making the process of your new home a pleasure. 

Cavalier Homes understands the importance of building a home that meets customers needs and 
lifestyle,  and is committed to delivering high quality results.  We value our customers,  and we aim to 
exceed their expectations. 

Cavalier Homes is currently targeting new areas in the market in the next 1  to 3 years,  specifically in 
relation to lower end homebuilders and government housing projects.

Detailed Plan 

Business Structure 

Cavalier Homes Gou I burn Valley Pty Ltd operates as a Company for the following reasons: 

•  Taxation rates are more favourable in a company 
•  Selling the business will be easier by selling shares in the future 
•  This structure allows the Shareholders to be employed by the company 
•  Liability for shareholders is limited 

The exit strategy is for the current Shareholder's to mentor key managers and to sell their shares in the 
next 5 to 10 Years.

Market Analysis 

Cavalier Homes Goulburn Valley has undertaken extensive market research analysis in relation to the 
building industry environment,  sales targets and competitors. 

In the current financial climate, with a number of interest rate increases in the past 12 months, the 
construction industry is experiencing a difficult and uncertain period.  Cavalier homes sets themselves 
apart from other builders by having the necessary resources to overcome the current difficulties faced in 
the construction industry. 

Industry Analysis 

Cavalier Homes has undertaken extensive market research through reviewing trends and statistics in 
relation the building and construction industry.  The building and construction industry is the most 
important sector in Victoria, delivering significant employment,  Gross State Product (GSP) and taxation 
revenue.  The construction sector is the third largest employer in the state, generating about 358,900 
jobs and $39.6 billion (2021/22) in gross added value. 

In recent time the building and construction industry has experienced a significant down turn due to 
rising interest rates,  soaring cost of living and a shortages in labour and raw materials. Total dwelling 
commencements fell 10.4% and the value of total building work done fell 0.1 % to $34.3 billion.  This is 
reflected with a number of builders being forced out of the industry in the Goulburn Valley area, which 
has in turn increased the market share significantly of Cavalier Homes Shepparton. 

The Australian government is currently exploring initiatives to support the construction industry and 
encourage construction of affordable housing to address the shortage of affordable homes in the 
country.  This has resulted in an increase in investment opportunities in the residential construction 
sector which Cavalier Homes has recognised and is a target market.

Target Market Analysis 

Cavalier Homes target market are predominantly homeowners with minimal investors. The Cavalier 
Homes target market has evolved over the years from first home buyers to their current target market, 
being middle aged people with families and who are at the mid to high income level  bracket.  This 
market is focused on custom design homes and wanting to build a beautiful family forever home.  This 
target group make up the majority of Cavalier Homes market share, however, Cavalier homes have 
recognised a need for the lower end market. 

Due to the increased interest rates and cost of living, Cavalier Homes has recognised through research, 
the lack of affordability for first home buyers.  Cavalier Homes recognise first home buyers are struggling 
to build their first home not only due to increased interest and living costs, but also due to the scepticism 
surround the building and construction industry and therefore they aren't willing to commit to a builder. 
To combat this,  Cavalier Homes goal within the next twelve months is to build speculative homes on the 
low end to be sold to the targeted market group of first home buyers. 

Cavalier Homes Goulburn Valley are looking to target increases sales in the Moama NSW region where 
there is more land available as opposed to the Shepparton area where land is becoming more scarce. 

Cavalier Homes has identified through research the current shortage for social housing in Victoria and 
was successful in applying for a women's housing tender to build 9 homes in the Goulburn Valley 
Region.  This is an area of the market sector that Cavalier Homes will continue to target and the objective 
is to secure 1-2 government social housing tenders per year. 

Competitors Analysis 

Cavalier Homes Goulburn Valley have the following direct competitors: 

•  JG King Homes 

•  GJ Gardiner 

•  Cirillo Homes 

In the last 12 months due to difficulties faced in the building industry, two of Cavalier Homes main 
competitors being Metricon and Dennis Family Homes have suffered a huge downturn in their market 
share and are no longer are a direct competitor.  Due to the financial downturn of these businesses, this 
has led to an increase in Cavalier Homes market share. 

Based on home build statistics in the Goulburn Valley,  Cavalier Homes hold approximately a 20% market 
share with JG King holding 20% and the rest are below this percentage.  Given two major competitors 
have exited and Cavalier Homes is targeting new areas in Government social housing, we aim to take at 
least 25% of the market share by the end of 2026.

IT Cability 

Cavalier Homes Goulburn Valley utilise three software programs for their estimating, accounting and site 
management needs. 

•  Estimating Software - Cavalier Homes utilise Databuild software which is a very powerful and 
capable tool for the estimating, job costing, and tracking of house build stage completions. 
Databuild also includes the accounting tools required, including accounts payable & receivable 
and financial information,  however this part of the software is outdated and cumbersome. 
Databuild does not include payroll. 

•  Accounting - As mentioned above Databuild is quite tedious in relation to the accounting side of 
the business.  It is difficult to obtain financial information in a timely manner as information needs 
to be extracted and collated in other programs such as excel.  Cavalier Homes Goulburn Valley 
recognises this weakness and has already implemented the introduction of Xero for payroll and 
the goal is to implement accounts payable/receivable and financial reporting across to Xero by 1st 
July,  2024.  This will assist management in having access to financial reporting in a timely manner 
and to make decisions when needed. 

•  Site Management - Companion systems is a powerful software tool used by Supervisors onsite to 

track the stages of house builds,  recording of tasks completed and to determine forecasted 
completion. The software guides and prompts supervisors on forecasted tasks to build the home 
in the timeframe set. This software is also used by administration staff to view where the job is at 
any particular point in-time, assist with forecast income reporting, and also is a database that 
includes full document management, from contracts to house designs and home warranty 
insurance.
