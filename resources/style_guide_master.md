### Typography
**Primary Font:** `Avant Garde Gothic` (Book for Headlines, Medium for Body)
**Secondary Font:** `Playfair Display` (Headings)
**Substitute:** `Century Gothic` (If primary unavailble)

## 4. Tone of Voice Rules
- **House & Land:** Lifestyle-focused but factual. No price anchoring.
- **Investment:** Neutral, conservative. No "High Return" claims.
- **Display Homes:** Experience-led. Distinguish upgrades.
- **Fast Keys:** Simplified, Reassuring, Efficient.
