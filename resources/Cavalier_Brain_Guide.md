# 🧠 Cavalier Brain: User Guide

Welcome to the **Cavalier Brain**, your project's central intelligence hub. This system allows you to store documents, meetings, and research in one place and use AI to ask questions, generate summaries, and find insights instantly.

## 1. Getting Started

### Accessing the Brain
1.  Navigate to the **CavalierOne Dashboard**.
2.  Click on the **"Brain"** link in the main navigation menu.
3.  You will see the main interface with "Notebooks" on the left and the "Chat" area on the right.

---

## 2. Managing Notebooks

Think of a **Notebook** as a folder for a specific topic (e.g., "Marketing Strategy", "Site #23 Feasibility", "Competitor Research").

-   **Create a New Notebook**:
    1.  Click the **+ New Notebook** button on the bottom left.
    2.  Enter a descriptive title (e.g., "Q1 Update").
    3.  The new notebook will appear in your list and automatically be selected.

-   **Switching Notebooks**:
    -   Simply click on any name in the **Notebooks** list to switch contexts. The AI will only answer questions based on the selected notebook's content.

---

## 3. Adding Knowledge (Sources)

You can feed the Brain information from various sources so it can answer your questions correctly.

### A. Web Pages & YouTube
1.  Copy the URL of a website, article, or YouTube video.
2.  Paste it into the **"Add Source"** box at the bottom right.
3.  Click **"Add Source"**.
4.  *Tip: For YouTube, the Brain reads the transcript/captions, so it "knows" what was said in the video.*

### B. Meeting Notes (Fireflies.ai Integration)
Transform your spoken conversations into searchable knowledge.

**Step 1: Record a Meeting**
-   **Download the App**: Get the **Fireflies.ai** app for [iOS](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/fireflies-ai-meeting-notes/id1527848601) or [Android](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.fireflies.android).
-   **Log In**: Sign in with your Google account.
-   **Record**: Tap the **+ (Plus)** button in the app to record an in-person meeting, phone call, or site visit notes.
-   *Wait*: It takes a few minutes for Fireflies to transcribe the audio after you stop recording.

**Step 2: Import to Brain**
1.  Go to the **Cavalier Brain** dashboard.
2.  Look at the **"Fireflies Meetings"** list on the bottom left.
3.  Find your meeting (e.g., "Site Walkthrough - 12 Feb").
4.  Click **"Import to Brain"**.
5.  The full transcript is now part of your selected Notebook!

---

## 4. Chatting with Your Data

Once you have added sources (websites, videos, or meetings), you can ask the AI anything.

-   **Ask Questions**: "What was the budget mentioned in the meeting?" or "Summarize the key selling points from that YouTube video."
-   **Generate Content**: "Write a follow-up email based on the site walkthrough notes."
-   **Combine Info**: "Based on the competitor website and our meeting notes, what allows us to win this deal?"

### Tips for Best Results
-   **Be Specific**: Instead of "Summarize this," try "Summarize the action items assigned to Jason."
-   **Check the Source**: The AI is grounded in the documents you provide. If it doesn't know the answer, make sure you've added the relevant source!
